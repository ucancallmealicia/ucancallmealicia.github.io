I'm a metadata archivist in the Manuscripts and Archives Department at Yale University Library. I develop methods to create, modify, and analyze archival data in bulk. I also build tools and develop procedures to make archival work more efficient.


I am especially interested in how archivists can employ computational methods to make the most of our limited resources, and to facilitate the broadest possible access to our materials. These interests are informed by a background studying history and philosophy, and working as a processing and reference archivist.
