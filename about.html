I'm a metadata archivist in the
Manuscripts and Archives Department
at Yale University.


In my job I use a variety of methods
to create, update, and analyze archival data in bulk.
I also build tools and develop procedures
to make archival work more efficient.
And I sometimes process archival collections.


I am especially interested in how archivists
can leverage scripting languages and
other technologies to make the most of
our limited time time and resources.


This is my website, where I keep
some of my professional work,
especially my coding projects,
writing, and presentations.
